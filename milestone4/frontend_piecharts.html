A Sentiment Analysis of Big Tech

In this project, we present an analysis of a corpus of tweets / blogs about four Big Tech companies: Apple, Amazon, Facebook, and Microsoft.

In particular, we look at how they are perceived on social media, drawing from English and French tweets on Twitter and Chinese blog posts on Weibo. The goal of this project is to understand how consumers perceive these companies and whether there are noticeable differences across languages and countries. We hope that you find these results interesting and enlightening.

Happy exploring!

How to use this corpus:

There are three ways to access this corpus. Under 'Results Type', You can pick one of HTML table, bar chart, or pie chart.

If you pick Table, you can input a search term into the Keyword Search box below, and apply as many filters as you wish. If you do not choose any of the filters, the default is that all languages, companies, and sentiments will be included.

If you pick Bar Chart, three bar charts will appear. If you pick Pie Chart, the resulting visualizations will be organized according to company, and you can use the buttons at the top to toggle between them.
